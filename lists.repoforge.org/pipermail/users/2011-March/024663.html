It's not broken, it's generated under RHEL6 and therefore uses SHA1
hashes which RHEL5 does not support.

Either get the spec from here:

http://svn.rpmforge.net/svn/trunk/rpms/lsyncd/

or you will have to use --nomd5 or --nofiledigest to extract the SRPM.
