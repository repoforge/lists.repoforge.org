http://www.mirrorservice.org/sites/apt.sw.be/redhat/el5/en/i386/dag/RPMS/lftp-3.5.14-1.el5.rf.i386.rpm:
[Errno -1] Header is not complete. Trying other mirror.

All this error is telling me is that the mirror you're downloading the header
from doesn't have the correct header.

Just do a "yum clean all" and retry, which would go to another mirror. If not,
then change your yum config to use a mirror you know works or try direct from
rpmforge.
