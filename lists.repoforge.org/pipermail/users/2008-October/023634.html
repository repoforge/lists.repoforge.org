Hi Thomas

rpmforge does not update base packages of RHEL/CentOS. Also the version
of software in CentOS has bug- and securityfixes backported,
https://rhn.redhat.com/errata/rhel-server-errata.html lists one.
The idea of "enterprise distributions" is not to always ship the latest
realease but to incorporate no big changes/new versions.
