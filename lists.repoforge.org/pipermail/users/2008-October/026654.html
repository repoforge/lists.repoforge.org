Thanks for the feedback. The problem with this kind of software is that 
everything depends on the media. While for some people it may be worse, 
for others it may be an improvement and so you cannot make a decision 
based on one or even on multiple reports (unless there is a consistent 
line of nothing working ;-))

In the case an older version works better for your use scenario, I can 
only recommended to downgrade back to that level and try again when 
another version is released.

And more importantly, report your problems to the upstream project rather 
than here, especially if it is not a packaging problem in itself.
