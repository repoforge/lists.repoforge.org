As Seth once explained to me, yum satisfies its dependencies from the
package with the shortest name, because otherwise you could end up
installing some .debuginfo-package instead of the package you want to
install. And that's 16 letters in perl-Archive-Tar vs. 13 in
perl-CPANPLUS.
