yes, I have had this too and my solution has been to disable the library
with cfengine for our laptop users.

You can fix it like this:

 # mv /usr/lib64/libtag.so.1 /usr/lib64/libtag.so.1.disable

and it will work again. It happens regularly, I get messages from cfengine
saying it has fixed it (again), but at least it does not bother me or my
colleagues :-)
