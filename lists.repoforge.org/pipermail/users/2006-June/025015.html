Good evening, all,
 	The razor-agents package used to have 4 binaries in /usr/bin 
(razor-admin, razor-check, razor-report, and razor-revoke) as symlinks to 
razor-client, the script that did the real work.  As of razor-agents 2.81 
(and possibly earlier), razor-client no longer does real work - it's just 
a dummy script.  For that reason, the other 4 binaries shouldn't be 
symlinks to it.
 	I believe the following three lines in the razor-agents-spec file 
can be removed:

for bin in razor-check razor-report razor-admin razor-revoke; do
     %{__ln_s} -f razor-client %{buildroot}%{_bindir}/$bin
done
