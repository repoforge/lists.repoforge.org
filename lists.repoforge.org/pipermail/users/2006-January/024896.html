There is an ongoing on and off again thread on this.  Check a posting 
by Ralf Ertzinger on 9-March, 2005 where he was so kind to provide the 
workaround.  That patch was applied for a time in the repository, but 
then Matthias decided to yank it - see my post on 9-November.

In the meantime the situation is that you have alot of people who are 
just re-applying Ralf's patch manually until someone gets around to 
fixing dvdbackup.  Probably not the most efficient use of everyones 
time, but at least a workaround is available.
