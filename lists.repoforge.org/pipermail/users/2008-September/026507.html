The site of streamdvd doesn't exist anymore and the last version of dvdcreate 
is released several years ago. They both need some changes/patches so they 
compile against the current libdvdread library. You can check this in the 
buildlogs at 
http://dag.wieers.com/rpm/packages/dvdbackup/_buildlogs/dvdbackup-0.1.1-1.el5.rf.i386.ko.log.gz
http://dag.wieers.com/rpm/packages/streamdvd/_buildlogs/streamdvd-0.4-2.el5.rf.i386.ko.log.gz

Dag: the faq at http://openfacts.berlios.de/index-en.phtml?title=lxdvdrip 
doesn't mention streamdvd and dvdbackup anymore as required programs => 
simply remove the requirements?
