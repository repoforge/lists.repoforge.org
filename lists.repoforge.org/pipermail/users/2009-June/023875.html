Hello,

There seem to be a few packages that need WWW::Shorten to function, but
do not have the appropriate dependency. The three packages (that I
found) are:

* perl-POE-Component-WWW-Shorten
* perl-WWW-Shorten-0rz
* perl-WWW-Shorten-KUSO

I've included detailed information below. I would like to request that
WWW::Shorten is added to the repository and that the dependencies are
added to the packages.

Thank you,
Matt
