Hi,
perl-IO-Compress-2.030-2 does require perl(Compress::Raw::Zlib) >=
2.024. Do update all the three packages:
perl-Compress-Raw-Bzip2
perl-Compress-Raw-Zlib
perl-IO-Compress

So:
yum clean all; yum update perl-Compress-Raw-Bzip2 perl-Compress-Raw-Zlib
perl-IO-Compress
