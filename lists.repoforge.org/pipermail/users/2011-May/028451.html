I assume you have a dependency already installed coming from EPEL or 
another source. Probably ffmpeg, as I didn't see it in your output and 
it seems to be the cause.

There is an RPMforge mailinglist to discus such problems:

     http://lists.rpmforge.net/mailman/listinfo/users

but in this case it's caused by mixing dependencies from 
different (incompatible) repositories.
