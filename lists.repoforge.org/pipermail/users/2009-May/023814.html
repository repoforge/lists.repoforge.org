Hello.

There is a problem at the mod_suphp rpm. It has a settings file
/etc/suphp.conf of old format.
Handlers section should have it's parameters quoted:
[handlers]
;Handler for php-scripts
x-httpd-php="php:/usr/bin/php-cgi"

;Handler for CGI-scripts
x-suphp-cgi="execute:!self"


In the current version they are unquoted.
