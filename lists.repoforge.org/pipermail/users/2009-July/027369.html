Well, basically I've already shared my current solution with you. I have
my packages uploaded at http://rpm.zaytsev.net, but first off, they are

compilation process if you'd later try to compile something curl-based
yourself. 

I've just tried to do a static build of rtorrent for x86_64 and here you
go: http://rpm.zaytsev.net/rtorrent.tar.bz2 . I don't have any other
clean EL5 x86_64 boxes to test it, so I need your help.

                 libsigc++20 openssl ncurses
