I plan to drop FC1, FC2 and FC3 this christmas. (Dries is doing the more

announcement only stresses that if you plan to stick with a certain Fedora 
release for too long, you're on your own.

packages. But I could make use of the saved resources (diskspace, 
metadata creation, maintenance) and the extra time.
