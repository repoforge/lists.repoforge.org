I took the 2.08 spec from Fedora Extras 5

 
ftp://download.fedora.redhat.com/pub/fedora/linux/extras/5/SRPMS/libapre
q2-2.08-1.fc5.src.rpm

added 

  BuildRequires:  perl(Tie::IxHash)

and ever since then it has worked like a charm for me on CentOS4 i386
and CentOS5 x86_64

On EL4 you'll also have to build mod_perl because perl-libapreq2
requires mod_perl >= 2.0.0-0.rc5 and EL4 only provides 1.99. I solved
this problem by rebuilding mod_perl-2.0.3-1.el4s1.3.src.rpm from the
centosplus repo.
