The problem reported earlier this year (July, August 2005) regarding 
transcode-1.0.0-1.2.fc4 appears to have been corrected in 
transcode-1.0.2-2.2.fc4.

The problem was related to AMD Athlon XP chips and the use of the 
default or specifying SSE or SSE2 as an option.  The workaround was 
override the default by specifying the option:  "--accel 3dnow".  All 
appear to work fine now.
