Also I would suggest adding this to /etc/httpd/conf.d/trac.conf:

<Directory "/usr/share/trac/htdocs/">
         AuthType Basic
         AuthName "trac"
         AuthUserFile /usr/share/trac/trac.htpasswd
         Require valid-user
</Directory>
