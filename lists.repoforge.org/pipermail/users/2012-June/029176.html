It's best not to mix and match repositories, especially for things like 
a mail stack.

I'd suggest you also use perl-Razor-Agent from repoforge (rpmforge), not 
EPEL.

I tend to stick with repoforge, and then *only* go to other 3rd party 
repos for packages which are *not* in repoforge.

Hope this helps.
