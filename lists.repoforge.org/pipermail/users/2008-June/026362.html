Greg Bailey wrote:
> I'm running CentOS 5.2 and notice that I now have the yum-fastestmirror  
> plugin, which works great; except that most of the time, I get:
>
> Determining fastest mirrors
> * rpmforge: mirror.cpsc.ucalgary.ca
>
> For whatever reason, I get REALLY slow download rates from that  
> particular server.  Also, I frequently get checksum errors if I get the  
> repomd.xml file from that server, and then CTRL-C to get a different  
> (faster) mirror--the other mirrors don't seem to match the repomd.xml  
> from ucalgary.ca.

Wrong list for that, but try to put 

127.0.0.2 mirror.cpsc.ucalgary.ca

into your /etc/hosts.

After that clear timedhosts.txt out of /var/cache/yum and try again.

Cheers,

Ralph
