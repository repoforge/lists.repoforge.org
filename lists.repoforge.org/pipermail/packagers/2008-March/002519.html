To follow up on the nagios 3 packages (though I wasn't on this list at
the time), I've looked at Christophe's packages and they work pretty
well. I've modified his spec file slightly to make it closer to the
original rpmforge nagios spec, and made a couple other minor changes
for compatibility. The perl switch for checkresults allows for an
upgrade from nagios 2.x to nagios 3.x without conflict.

In theory it SHOULD be transparent to the user. Comments and
suggestions welcome.
