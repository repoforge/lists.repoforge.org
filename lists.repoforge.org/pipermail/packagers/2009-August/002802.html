Here are my personal opinions on this topic:

- KISS: keep it simple stupid, try to avoid complex conditionals etc
- no absolute paths: filenames in the %files section should _ALWAYS_
start with a macro (e.g. %{_bindir}, %{_libdir} etc), exception %doc
entries
- avoid globs: in the %files section try to avoid using globs, on
updates new/missing files will be detected (i am also not a fan of the
filelist feature)
- try to use macros wherever possible, especially %{name} and %{version}
but also thinks like %{__make} and %configure
