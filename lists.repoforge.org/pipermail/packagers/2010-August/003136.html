Hi,

We occasionally get requests for mirrors and while I would like more 
mirrors, we never had a good set of rules or procedure to make this 
happen.

Looking at the tree:

 	[root at surya rpmforge]# du -sxh production
 	80G     production

 	[root at surya rpmforge]# du -sxh production/*
 	717M    production/aurora
 	13G     production/dries
 	27G     production/fedora
 	34G     production/redhat
 	14G     production/source

 	[root at surya rpmforge]# du -sxh production/redhat/*
 	343M    production/redhat/6.2
 	1.9G    production/redhat/7.3
 	981M    production/redhat/8.0
 	3.2G    production/redhat/9
 	1.5G    production/redhat/el2.1
 	5.8G    production/redhat/el3
 	11G     production/redhat/el4
 	12G     production/redhat/el5

Since I hope to get rid of the aurora and fedora trees (as they've become 
unmaintained) we can reduce RPMforge's size to below 50G.

But we need some answers to:

  - Do we allow mirrors to mirror only subsets ?

  - How do we monitor mirror consistency ?

  - How do we manage yum's mirrorlist ?

  - Are we going to provide subdomains for mirrors ?

I have no clue in mirror-management, so any feedback is welcome !
