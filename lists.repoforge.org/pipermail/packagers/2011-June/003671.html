Hi Michael,

Few notes regarding your submission:

1) Commenting macros (#%{...}) is generally a very bad idea. 

The reason for it is that this doesn't stop them from being expanded (%%
does), and you might run into lots of trouble where macros will be
defined where they shouldn't or expanded across multiple lines etc.

If it works with your SPEC right now, fine, but keep it in mind.

2) Our main repository is not hosted @ github and we gladly accept pull
requests: https://github.com/repoforge/rpms

3) Could you please comment on idoutils-init that we have in our
repository and that I no longer see referenced from the SPEC? 

Shall I nuke it then?
