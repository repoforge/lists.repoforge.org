I was wondering if there was any interest in an Australian mirror for RPMforge?

We're one of Australia's largest content networks and appear on every major peering point within Australia. Our network is IPv6 enabled along with our mirror servers (native dual stack, not with a tunnel) and we have 100Mbit available to our mirrors.
