Hi,

I worked on the repository scripts again and implemented support for the 
buildlogs (at least my own buildlog format for the time being, until it 
is standardized). That means that the buildlogs are currently available 
from, eg.:

 	http://packages.sw.be/smplayer/_buildlogs/

I also fixed one of the performance problems we had wrt. the metadata 
creation and reduced the time for making apt SRPM repositories from 15mins 
to 3mins per repository. (We have about 32 SRPM repositories) The fix to 
apt's genbasedir cachedir behaviour was send upstream.

More such fixes will be necessary to reduce metadata creation to something 
acceptable for us to be more flexible in updating repositories (now only 
once a day !).
