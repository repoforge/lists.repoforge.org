With the work that christoph has put into it, and the 1 or two lines
of mine, it should be a reasonably seemless upgrade. There are a few
minor variable changes, but I think the biggest concern would be that
some bugs are still creeping out of nagios 3.x. There are some bugs
about nagios not closing filehandles, forking for checks improperly,
double statements in the config files, and an assortment of typos in
the documentation.

Honestly I'd hold off just a little longer, unless there was a way to
tag it for testing only, or require manual download.
