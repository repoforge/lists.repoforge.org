+<h3>Video Controller :</h3>
+</p>
+
+<p>
+As of writing, Fedora Core 4 Test2 installed fine, but only with the vesa driver at 800x600, though. Recompiling xorg-x11 from CVS was required in order for the i810 driver to recognize the card and get accelerated display.<br/>
+I haven't been able to get the display to work at the native resolution of 1280x768, only 1024x768, and it looks quite ugly because of the horizontal stretching.
