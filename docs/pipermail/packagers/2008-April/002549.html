> I am currently a public mirror of the following: fc7, fc8, el4, el5
> (i386 and x86_64)
>  
> First I would need to need to know the footprint of the RPMforge mirror
> for those distros and what the expected bandwidth consumption would be
> during 7a-7p M-F. (My bandwidth requirements are considerable less in
> the evenings and weekends)

Footprint is in the region of 40G, if you have that much space, you 
should be ok. In terms of b/w usage expect to ship between 1.2 to 
1.8mbps on a sustained basis. Sometimes there are peaks of upto 3 to 7 
mbps but 95% percentile stays below 1.8mbps

I am not sure if there is an easy way to handle a partial mirror, but 
there is a need to setup some sort of a bouncer / mirror checker.
