On Tue, 2011-02-01 at 19:29 +0000, Jesse Hathaway wrote:

> Other than the mock suggestion by Erik, what other tools are helpful in the
> backporting process?

Actually, there are no special tools designed for use only in the
backporting process. Basically you need to have a good knowledge of the
package that you are working with, an IDE, source control tool and a
tool to rebuild packages in a clean environment to ensure the
reproducibility.

The first items are entirely a matter of habit (i.e. I do lots of small
backports just with mc), as for the latter, mock holds a monopoly here.
