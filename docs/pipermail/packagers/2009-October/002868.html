I'm very much in favor of putting macros in rpm-macros-rpmforge and 
having it be a requirement for building rpmforge packages.
What I'm objecting to is making it a BuildRequires in the specfile.
It's a basic requirement, in time all rpmforge packages may come to 
depend on it so it makes no sense that every single specfile have a 
redundant BuildRequires: rpm-macros-rpmforge.
