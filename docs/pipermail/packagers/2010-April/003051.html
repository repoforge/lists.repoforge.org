hi Guys,

There are 2 issues that would be good to resolve for the mirrors setup.

1) How do we check for 'update state' for each mirror that is signed up 
as being official ?

2) how do we then set that up to work from a mirrorlist= type setup on 
the server end ?

We have enough resources to allow, atleast some, of the people to mirror 
directly and we could setup some web based form that people can fill in 
to sign up. Its really a case of working out what happens from that 
point on and how mirrors are handed out to client's.

There are a couple of existing setups that might also be worth 
considering - eg: suse's mirrorbrain and fedora's mirrormanager.

Who's offering to take up the task of evaluating the options and coming 
up with recommendations and perhaps even helping with implementation ?
