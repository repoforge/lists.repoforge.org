I looked at the SPEC file, and the specific fc10 references ought to go (I 
need to test to see what would be the best way to do it based on 
capabilities rather than specific versions). The main reason is that it 
will become ugly with fc11, el6, fc12 and others in the picture :)

So the introduction of a _without_something macro is needed.

Regarding the EL4 builds, a quick looks indeed seems to reveal it is not 
possible unless we replace pango, or we get rid of the 
pango_cairo_context_set_font_options requirement in pango.

Without upstream acknowledging this is important for them (or at least a 
trivial patch that we can maintain) changes are slim that we will go 
forward with EL4 and older builds. Did you discuss this on the rrdtool 
mailinglist already ? Maybe they didn't know, or maybe they don't care :)
