On Aug 5, 2010, at 5:14 PM, Christoph Maser wrote:

> I did a basic spec for TracAccounManager. The upstream does not have a
> version so the only download URL available is
> http://trac-hacks.org/changeset/latest/accountmanagerplugin/trunk?old_path=/&filename=accountmanagerplugin/trunk&format=zip
> wich is a moving target. How shall i handle that? 


rather than pulling from trunk, pull from a specific branch and revision (it would be even nicer if they would use the TTB format in their repo), such as:

http://trac-hacks.org/changeset/7731/accountmanagerplugin/0.11?old_path=%2F&old=7731&format=zip

this gives you r7731 of the 0.11 branch, which will download as 'accountmanagerplugin_0.11-r7731.zip'.

then %{version} = 0.11 and %{release} = r7731-1, perhaps.

for extra future-proofing, download that zipfile and then put it on some webspace somewhere; there may be a good location under packages.sw.be for such things.
