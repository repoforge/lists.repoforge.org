If you mean with a newer version of DAR: normally Dag does a commit of all his 
changes to DAR. If you really want to be sure: you can check the scripts in 
the last released version.

If you mean with another program: there are other programs like pydar2 and 
mach which are also meant for creating rpms, but they are simply different 
and have other advantages, disadvantages and features. It's not possible to 
say that program XYZ is 'better' and fully obsoletes another program.
