this error, at least, appears to stem from an rrdtool change,  
documented here:

http://bugs.debian.org/cgi-bin/bugreport.cgi?bug=494547

i didn't start seeing this error until i updated rrdtool and perl- 
rrdtool to 1.3.7-1 this morning.

a patch that fixed the problem for me is here (the line numbers aren't  
quite right, i applied the patch by hand):

http://munin.projects.linpro.no/svn/munin/branches/debian/lenny/trunk/debian/patches/380-munin-graph-utf8.patch

the system perl package provides Encode (at least under RHEL4 and  
RHEL5), so the patch adds no additional dependencies.
