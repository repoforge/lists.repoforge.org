As I understand it, EPEL is primarily backports from Fedora to RHEL
and older versions of Fedora. It's useful, but package skew between
RPMforge and EPEL can get pretty confusing, especially when one of
them decides to re-factor a package, such as Nagios and its plugins,
into a different set of packages. (It drove me nuts when this happened
to nagios-plugins, which was a good idea but broke my Nagios setup
badly.)
