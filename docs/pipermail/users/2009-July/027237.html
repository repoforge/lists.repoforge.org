Chris,

Don't feel disappointed about what you are doing. The perl updates are 
important and the resolution problems are a temporary situation.

People should not use RPMforge for production systems anyway, they should 
cherry-pick and test those packages in their own repositories.

In fact, I think the main problem is that yum simply cannot handle this 
properly, it bails out and refuses to work, while apt-get is much better 
for this.
