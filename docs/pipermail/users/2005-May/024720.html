>    http://apt.sw.be/extra/state/packages*.list)

>  + Add new packages by sending SPEC-files and patches to:
>    suggest at lists.rpmforge.net
> 
>  + What we desperately need is someone driving progress by making TODO
>    lists, encouraging people to help, improve the website, ... This is
>    more like general management, less technical.

>  + If you're more into building and developing, you can help Dries with
>    his pydar development to make the best buildsystem in existence :)
