Hi Paul,

There are a few ways you can already help out. Here's a list of things:

 + Go over the SPEC files, ask questions about them and fix whatever can 
   be improved. Questions will help us adding/improving documents on te 
   website.

 + Go over the buildlogs, look for failed builds and see what can be done 
   about it. (see: http://apt.sw.be/packages/3ddesktop/_buildlogs/)
   Older distributions are not that important as EL3, FC3, EL4

 + Compare the packages lists to find broken builds (see: 
   http://apt.sw.be/extra/state/packages*.list)

 + Add new packages by sending SPEC-files and patches to: 
   suggest at lists.rpmforge.net

 + What we desperately need is someone driving progress by making TODO 
   lists, encouraging people to help, improve the website, ... This is 
   more like general management, less technical.

 + If you're more into building and developing, you can help Dries with 
   his pydar development to make the best buildsystem in existence :)

And there are probably plenty more things that needs a hand. So jump in, 
take something you like and we are here to help you if you need something.
