Hi there...

The dante socks client package (1.1.19-2) seems to have a bug, causing 
it to fail socksifying your system.

Changing:

[ ! -r /etc/socks.conf ] || disable
[ ! -x /lib/libdsocks.so.0 ] || disable

to

[ -r /etc/socks.conf ] || disable
[ -x /lib/libdsocks.so.0 ] || disable

in the dsocksify.sysv portion of the spec-file, fixes the problem.

/Thomas
