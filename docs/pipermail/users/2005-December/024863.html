thanks rex, i appreciate your help.  i am new to writing cron jobs and i 
have created a cron job as such:

---rsync.cron---
#!/bin/bash
rsync -ai4CH --delete --bwlimit 300 \ 
rsync://apt.sw.be/pub/freshrpms/pub/dag/ /pub/rpmforge/dag
---end rsync.cron---

(that command is all on one line.)

i added the "--bwlimit 300" to leave me some download bandwidth for 
other things, the "-4" to prefer ipv4, and the "-i" to give me a summary 
of changes.  you can tell i've been reading the rsync man page.  will 
that suffice?
