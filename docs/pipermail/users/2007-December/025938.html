On Fri, 7 Dec 2007, Max Kanat-Alexander wrote:

> 	Email::Send needs Module::Pluggable, but when I "up2date
> perl-Email-Send" from rpmforge, I don't get Module::Pluggable
> installed. This is on RHEL4 x86_64.

You can simply do this on your RHEL x86_64 system to verify what package
is satisfying your dependency:

	rpm -q --whatprovides 'perl(Module::Pluggable)'

However, looking at the perl-Email-Send package, I do not see a dependency
to perl(Module::Pluggable):

	[dag at lisse ~]# rpm -qpR perl-Email-Send-2.192-1.el4.rf.noarch.rpm
	perl >= 0:5.005
	perl(Email::Address)
	perl(Email::Simple)
	perl(File::Spec)
	perl(Net::NNTP)
	perl(Return::Value)
	perl(Scalar::Util)
	perl(Symbol)
	perl(strict)
	perl(vars)
	rpmlib(CompressedFileNames) <= 3.0.4-1
	rpmlib(PayloadFilesHavePrefix) <= 4.0-1
	rpmlib(VersionedDependencies) <= 3.0.3-1

Why do you think perl-Email-Send requires perl(Module::Pluggable) ?
