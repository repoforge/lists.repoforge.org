Sorry for the delay in getting back to you. I can see that I didn't
make things clear in my original message, the
'gstreamer-plugins-bad-free-extras' package (ver. 0.10.19-2.el6) comes
from the rhel-x86_64-server-optional-6 repository.

What I was attempting to highlight is that there is a way that a
conflict will occur between a package from the Red Hat repo and the
standard RPMforge repo.

To avoid the conflict, I removed
'gstreamer-plugins-bad-free-extras-0.10.19-2.el6.x86_64' and let
'gstreamer-plugins-bad-0.10.19-3.el6.rf.x86_64' take precedence.
