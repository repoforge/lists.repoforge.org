I updated zaptel to 1.2.3 and 1.2.4, respectively, and built them
on FC4 x86_64.  My spec files, SRPMs, and RPMs may be found at:

    http://www.brouhaha.com/~eric/software/fedora/fc4/asterisk/

I had to change a 'grep' in the build section of the zaptel spec
to avoid a problem with RPM wanting to package /var/tmp/zaptel*/var
which doesn't exist:

<     grep -v 'dir /dev$' > device.list
---
>     grep /dev/zap > device.list

I also had to undo a change made by Matthias on 25-Nov-2005 which
says that kernel modules are now in "extra" rather than "misc", as
they still seem to build in "misc" for me.

One thing I did NOT fix is that the Makefile assumes that restorecon
is in the current path, while I don't usually have /sbin or /usr/sbin
in my path.
